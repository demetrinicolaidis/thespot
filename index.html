About The Spot!

We are a buy, sell trade store focusing on streetwear style shoes, apparel, accessories, and more! We buy and sell popular footwear brands like Jordan, Yeezy, Nike, Asics, New Balance, and more. Our apparel section features brands from Supreme to Dior to Louis Vuitton and everything in between. All items are 100% authenticated.


Our inventory is constantly rotating, so come by the store to see what we've got in stock along with new pickups!

Our Story

The Spot was founded by Connor Rabin in 2022. He is the sole owner of the business and makes the shop his work, day in and day out. Connor loves being located downtown so The Spot can serve the community directly.
